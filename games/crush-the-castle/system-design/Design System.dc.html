Responsive
Desktop1920 × 1080
  • HUD pinned to screen corners; play-field scales to fit (FIT scale mode).
  • Power meter centred along the bottom safe-area.
  • Generous spacing scale (24–64px) for breathing room.
Tablet1024 × 768
  • Controls grow to ≥ 64px hit targets; meter docks bottom-right by the thumb.
  • Title/heading sizes step down ~15%; corner padding tightens to 16px.
  • Touch targets never below 44 × 44px — see swatch right.
Min touch target
44 × 44px (visual ≥ 30px + padding)
Use Phaser Scale.FIT with a 1920×1080 design resolution and CENTER_BOTH. Anchor HUD elements to camera viewport corners, not world coordinates, so they hold position across aspect ratios.
